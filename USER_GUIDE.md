# Visitor Check-In — User Guide

This guide is for visitors using the self-service check-in kiosk.

---

## What Is This?

The kiosk is a self-service check-in system. When you arrive at the office, tap the screen to let your host know you're here — no need to speak to a receptionist.

---

## Checking In

### Step 1 — Wake the Kiosk

Tap anywhere on the idle screen to begin.

> *[Screenshot: Idle/welcome screen with company logo and background]*

---

### Step 2 — Select Who You Are Visiting

You will see a list of employees organised alphabetically. Tap a **letter** at the top to jump to names beginning with that letter, then tap your host's name.

> *[Screenshot: Letter picker with alphabet buttons and employee list below]*

If you are not sure of the exact spelling, scroll through the list or try an adjacent letter.

---

### Step 3 — Expected Guests

If your host has added you to the expected guests list, your name will appear as a button on the next screen.

> *[Screenshot: Expected guests screen showing name button(s) and "Other Guest" button]*

**Tap your name** to pre-fill your details and go straight to confirmation.

**Tap "Other Guest"** if your name is not listed, or if you are a walk-in visitor — you will fill out a short form instead.

---

### Step 4 — Fill In Your Details

If you are filling in the form manually, enter:

| Field | Required? | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| First Name | Yes | |
| Last Name | Yes | |
| Company / Organisation | No | Leave blank if visiting personally |
| Expected Stay | No | Approximate hours and minutes |

> *[Screenshot: Check-in form with name, company, and stay duration fields]*

Tap **Check In** when done.

---

### Step 5 — Confirmation

A confirmation screen will appear briefly, then the kiosk returns to the idle screen. Your host has been notified automatically.

> *[Screenshot: Thank-you / confirmation screen]*

---

## Tips

- **Returning visitor?** The system may remember your company from a previous visit and fill it in automatically.
- **Wrong host selected?** Tap **Back** on any screen to return to the previous step.
- **Nothing happening?** Tap the screen — it may be in sleep mode.

---

## Privacy

Your name and company are recorded for security and building access purposes. Visit logs are retained for up to 365 days and then automatically deleted. Photos, if enabled, are captured for identification purposes only and stored securely on-site.
